# EuroTech Manufacturing GmbH Case

EuroTech Manufacturing GmbH is a mid-size German manufacturer with 1,200 employees and EUR180M revenue. It produces precision components for the automotive and renewable energy sectors.

A major automotive OEM representing 30% of revenue requires all Tier-1 suppliers to demonstrate full AI-enabled predictive quality management within 18 months or face contract termination.

At the same time, a fast-growing wind turbine manufacturer offers a partnership opportunity that could replace the at-risk automotive revenue, but requires significant retooling and hiring.

EuroTech must decide how to respond while considering financial constraints, workforce implications, regulatory requirements, supply chain dependencies, market timing, technology feasibility, and operational disruption.
